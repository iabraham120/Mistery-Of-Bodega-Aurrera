# 🛒 Mystery of Bodega Aurrera

Welcome to the mysterious and bureaucratic world of **Mystery of Bodega Aurrera**! This is a short indie game developed as a final project for a programming course. It is built entirely in **C** using the **Allegro** library.

## 📝 Storyline

You play as an average employee at Bodega Aurrera (essentially the Mexican Walmart 🏛️💚). Your boss promised you a well-deserved raise, only to cancel it the very next day with the classic promise: "I'll give it to you tomorrow." Welcome to the endless loop of corporate exploitation!

Your goal is to break this cycle and **retrieve your raise documents** before the week ends.

---

## 🎮 Game Mechanics

The game is played across **5 days**, divided into **two core phases**. The difficulty increases progressively each day.

### ☀️ Daytime Phase: Customer Service
* You must help the customers who visit the store.
* Currently features **two "dynamic" minigames** to complete your daily tasks.
* Once you help the required number of customers, your shift ends, and the real mission begins.

### 🌙 Nighttime Phase: The Infiltration
* Sneak into the store after hours to recover your raise files.
* **The Danger:** Your boss patrols the aisles. If he catches you, it's game over.
* **Distraction Mechanic:** You have a special item—a **coin**. Toss it to make noise, distract your boss, and alter his patrol route so you can slip past him.

* **Graphics Library:** Allegro (Handling graphics, audio, and keyboard inputs)
